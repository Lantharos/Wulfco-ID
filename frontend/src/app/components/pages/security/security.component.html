Security

PASSKEYS

Passkeys

RECOMMENDED

Passkeys are unique, highly secure tokens that provide quick and convenient access to your account or services. They act as a trusted key to unlock your account, often used in scenarios such as account recovery or emergency access. Passkeys are carefully managed and protected to ensure the utmost security of your account.
MULTI-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION

Security Keys

Security keys are a robust form of multi-factor authentication (MFA) that enhance account security. These physical devices, often in the form of USB tokens or NFC-enabled cards, provide an additional layer of protection. To access your account, you'll need to insert or tap the security key, proving your identity beyond a password.

Authenticator App

An authenticator app is a mobile application that generates time-based one-time passwords (TOTPs) for MFA. To log in, open the app, and it will display a unique code that changes periodically. Enter this code alongside your password to verify your identity and enhance your account's security.

OneTap

Onetap is a seamless and user-friendly MFA method that adds an extra layer of security to your account. When you attempt to log in, you receive a push notification on your mobile device. Simply tap to verify, and you're granted access, confirming that it's you trying to log in quickly and securely.

Email Authentication

Email authentication is a widely used MFA method that leverages your email account for added security. Upon login, you'll receive a one-time code via email. Enter this code along with your password to verify your identity and gain access to your account. It's a straightforward yet effective way to enhance account protection.
